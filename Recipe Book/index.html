White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Maida
  • Cheese
  • Herbs

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and add 1 spoon maida- stir till golden.
  3. Add milk slowly, whisking to avoid lumps.
  4. Add cheese, salt, pepper, oregano -mix well.
  5. Add boiled pasta, toss, and serve creamy!

Veg/Paneer Burger

Ingredients:

  • Burger buns
  • Aloo tikki/paneer
  • Cheese Slice
  • Mayo
  • Lettuce

Steps:

  1. Toast burger buns with butter.
  2. Shallow fry aloo tikki or paneer slices.
  3. Apply mayo on buns.
  4. Add tikki/paneer, cheese slice, and lettuce.
  5. Close and toast slightly till cheese melts.

Wrap Roll

Ingredients:

  • Roti
  • Egg/Paneer
  • Onion
  • Sauces
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook roti.
  2. Scramble egg or saute paneer with onion & spices.
  3. Place filling on roti with cheese and sauces.
  4. Roll it tight like a wrap.
  5. Toast for 1-2 mins on tawa for crispiness.

Street style Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Capsicum
  • Mayo
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Mix chopped onion, capsicum with mayo & spices.
  2. Spread mix on bread and top with cheese slices.
  3. Cover with another bread slice.
  4. Butter both sides and grill till golden.
  5. Cut in triangles, serve with ketchup.

Chocolate Cup Cake

Ingredients:

  • Maida
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Baking powder

Steps:

  1. In a cup, mix 2 tbsp maida + 1 tbsp cocoa + 1 tbsp sugar.
  2. Add 2 tbsp milk + pinch baking powder, stir to a smooth batter.
  3. Add choco chips or Nutella if you want!
  4. Microwave for 1.5-2 minutes.
  5. Let cool slightly & dig in with a spoon Chocolate.
